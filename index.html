Frequently Asked Questions

How fast is your 48-hour setup?

Day 0: Fit-check call. Hour 2: Technical setup begins. Hour 24: Testing and training. Hour 48: Live and monitoring. Most systems are answering calls within 48 hours of your "yes."

Do I need to integrate with other platforms?

We work with your existing phone, website, calendar, email, CRM, and payment systems. Our platform handles the heavy lifting while staying connected to your current workflow.

Who owns my customer data?

You do. All leads, conversations, and customer data remain yours. We provide the automation tools; you keep full ownership and can export data anytime.

Are you compliant with opt-out regulations?

Yes. All SMS and email automation includes proper opt-out mechanisms and follows TCPA, CAN-SPAM, and other applicable regulations. HIPAA compliance available for healthcare.

What happens after business hours?

AI systems work 24/7. Missed calls get immediate text responses, chatbots capture leads, and urgent calls can route to your emergency line. You choose the after-hours behavior.

Do I need to approve content before it goes out?

You control the approval workflow. Review templates during setup, then choose auto-send for routine communications or require approval for sensitive content like review responses.

Can I keep my current phone number?

Absolutely. We set up call forwarding so your existing number works exactly the same. Customers call the same number they always have—they just get better service.

What if I'm not satisfied with results?

Month-to-month means no long-term commitment. We provide weekly optimization for Pro clients and monthly reporting for all plans so you can track ROI and make informed decisions.

Do you cover all of NYC?

Yes, but we specialize in Staten Island and Brooklyn businesses. We understand the unique needs of these markets and provide local, hands-on support that bigger providers can't match.
